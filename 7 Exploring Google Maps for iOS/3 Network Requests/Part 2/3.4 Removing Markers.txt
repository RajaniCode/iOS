3.4 Removing Markers

When removing markers from your map, what is it important to do?

[Single Answer]

Make sure to nil out the map on the marker and the marker – in that order.
Make sure to nil out the marker and the map property – in that order.
Remove the marker from your NSSet.
Create a new NSSet with your markers.


Answer: Make sure to nil out the map on the marker and the marker – in that order.
