7.7 Constraints and Scroll Views

What kind of constraint should you add to make sure a scroll view will scroll horizontally?

[Single Answer]

A constraint on the contentSize property of the scroll view.
A constraint on the view that's closest to the bottom of the scroll view.
A scroll constraint on the scroll view.
It doesn't matter - you still need to set the contentSize in code.


Answer: A constraint on the view that's closest to the bottom of the scroll view.
