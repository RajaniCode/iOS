1.10 Tab Bar Image Tint Color

If you're adding an image to a tab bar and want the actual image to show up instead of an alpha mask, which rendering mode would you choose?

[Single Answer]

UIImageRenderingModeAlwaysOriginal
UIImageRenderingModeAlwaysTemplate
UIImageRenderingModeAlwaysAlpha
UIImageRenderingModeAlwaysImage


Answer: UIImageRenderingModeAlwaysOriginal
