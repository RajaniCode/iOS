4.4 Interactive Transitions and Gesture Ended

What type of action should you include in the UIGestureRecognizerStateEnded state of a gesture recognizer attached to an interactive transition?

[Single Answer]

Update the percent state of a transition that's in progress.
Call the finishInteractiveTransition or cancelInteractiveTransition method.
Call the dismissViewControllerAnimated: or presentViewController:animated: method.


Answer: Call the finishInteractiveTransition or cancelInteractiveTransition method.
