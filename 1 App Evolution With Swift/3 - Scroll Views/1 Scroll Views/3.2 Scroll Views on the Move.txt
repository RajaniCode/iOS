3.2 Scroll Views on the Move

What's one reason to move the scroll view in the storyboard outside of the view controller hierarchy?

[Single Answer]

You can independently adjust the height of the scroll view to be able to visually lay out all of your content.
You can reuse the scroll view in any other view controller.
You can only add subviews on the scroll view after you move it outside of the view controller hierarchy.


Answer: You can independently adjust the height of the scroll view to be able to visually lay out all of your content.
