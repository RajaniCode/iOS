2.4 Wait, Did I Connect This Outlet Yet?

Which of the following is a UI indicator in Xcode that an outlet is connected?

[Single Answer]

The object name in the storyboard scene editor appears in bold.
A line appears onscreen between the storyboard object and variable in the Swift file.
The variable has the text @IBOutlet in front of the line where it is defined.
The line where the variable appears in displays a darkened dot.


Answer: The line where the variable appears in displays a darkened dot.
